Please carefully consider the following instructions:

  • To fill an answer box, drag elements from the box on the left to the answer boxes on the right.

  • To clear an answer box, press the Clear button.

  • To delete an element (e.g. a dragged try) just click once with the pointer on the element.

  • To save each of your answers, please, always press the Save button; otherwise, your answers will not be valid!

  • To check your program for syntax errors, press the Format button and then press the Check button in the end of the form.


Please, take care of the handling of your code, because we know that the methods to be called can lead to application crashes! When you fill an answer box, please do not forget to press the Save button. Finally, when you are happy with your answers, just press the log-out link (up and right in the survey). Thank you!
