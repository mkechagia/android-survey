Welcome!

Survey scope:

Our lab is working on a research project aiming to identify how Android developers write code, in terms of software quality, readability, and robustness, for improving the stability of mobile apps. If you are an Android or Java developer, we are interested in getting information regarding your coding experience. In this survey, we give you a Java class of a real Android app and we ask you to handle seven experimental methods from the Android platform's API so that you can increase the app's stability. We would greatly appreciate your help if you could sign up and answer the survey. We recognize both your time and assistance. Your answers will be anonymized and the survey will be only for academic purposes. Due to a strict deadline, the survey will be available until Saturday, 15 Oct. 2016 (23:59 GMT). For any questions please send an email to: mkechagia[at]aueb.gr.
